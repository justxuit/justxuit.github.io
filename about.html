Hi, I'm Xuan.



For all those wondering, it's pronounced like "Schwann" cells (shout-out to all the biology fans out there).

In my transition from front-end developer to experience designer, I've focused on how design-thinking and empathy for the end user can translate to innovation and improvements in the digital space.

I believe that in order to achieve really great design, we must first take the time to understand the experiences, behaviors, and emotions that drive our users.

As a Master's student of HCI at the University of Michigan, I aim to further develop my skills as both a designer and coder. I look for opportunities to grow not only as an experience designer, but also as a creative thinker and an effective communicator.

In my free time, I enjoy baking desserts, losing track of time inside comic book stores, gaming (of both the video and board varieties), and chilling with my corgi.

You can always find me on LinkedIn, check out my resume, or shoot me an email.
