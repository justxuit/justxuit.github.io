MoNo Makeup

Roles: Product Designer, Graphic Designer

Duration: 1 month

Technology: Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma

Brand identity design for a unisex makeup brand, MoNo.

Designed to foster individuality and creativity, our product, branding, and marketing collateral seek to challenge the ideas of traditional gender norms.

Brand Mission

Celebrate Individuality



Identity & Values

Self-Expression

MoNo seeks to promote self-expression of the individual. Its products are an outlet for users to express their creativity and imagination.

Impactful Imagery

The brand's visual message reflects its mission to challenge established gender norms and definiitions.

Total Inclusion

Anyone and everyone is welcome. The MoNo brand seeks to empower everyone to pursue their own version of beauty.
