From 2017 to 2019, I worked as a front end developer at a company called LoveBook. LoveCoups.com is LoveBook's sister website that offers personalized, fully customizable printed coupon booklets. At the beginning of 2017, LoveCoups lacked a mobile-friendly version of their website. As all customization of this product is done from the site, they could not capitalize on the growing audience of users who primarily browse the internet with mobile devices. I was tasked with rebuilding the LoveCoups website based on branding guidelines and mockups provided by the LoveBook graphics team.
